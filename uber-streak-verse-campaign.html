Campaign idea

Every ride moves the user closer to a visible reward.

The Uber Streak-Verse uses goal-gradient psychology: the closer commuters get to the 10th trip, the stronger their motivation becomes. Instead of comparing apps at every booking moment, users return to Uber because progress is already waiting.

Hook: Every 10th trip earns you the 11th on us.

A simple promise that is easy to remember, easy to track, and powerful enough to shift daily commute behavior.
